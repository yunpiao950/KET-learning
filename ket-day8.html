📖 Part 2: 语法知识点 (5 分钟)

问路与指路 (Asking for and Giving Directions)

句型例子
询问地点Excuse me, where is the...? / How can I get to the...?
直走Go straight on. / Go straight ahead.
左转/右转Turn left. / Turn right.
在...旁边It's next to the bank. / It's near the park.
💡 小贴士：
  • 问路前要说 Excuse me 表示礼貌
  • left 和 right 既是方向词，也可以作形容词
  • between 用于两者之间，among 用于三者或以上

🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

How to Get to My House

Hi! I'm Jack. Let me tell you how to get to my house. First, take the bus to Green Street. Then, go straight on Green Street. Turn left at the supermarket. You will see a park on your right. My house is next to the park. It's a white house with a red door. The library is between my house and the school. If you get lost, you can call me. My phone number is 123-4567.

📚 生词提示：street 街道 | corner 角落 | white 白色的 | door 门 | lost 迷路的

✍️ Part 4: 练习题 (10 分钟)

一、选择正确的介词 (5 题)

1. The park is ___ my house and the school. (A) next (B) between (C) near

2. Turn left ___ the corner. (A) at (B) in (C) on

3. The bank is ___ to the post office. (A) next (B) between (C) straight

4. Go straight ___ . (A) on (B) in (C) at

5. Is there a supermarket ___ here? (A) on (B) near (C) at

二、单项选择 (5 题)

6. Excuse me, where ___ the library? (A) am (B) is (C) are

7. How can I ___ to the park? (A) get (B) go (C) come

8. Turn ___ at the traffic lights. (A) left (B) straight (C) on

9. My mother works in a ___ . (A) park (B) hospital (C) school

10. I borrow books from the ___ . (A) supermarket (B) library (C) hospital

三、阅读理解 (5 题)

11. How does Jack's friend go to his house? (A) By bike (B) By bus (C) By car

12. What street should they go to first? (A) Red Street (B) Green Street (C) Blue Street

13. Where should they turn left? (A) At the park (B) At the supermarket (C) At the library

14. What color is Jack's house door? (A) White (B) Red (C) Blue

15. What is between Jack's house and the school? (A) The park (B) The library (C) The supermarket
