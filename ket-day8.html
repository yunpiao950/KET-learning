🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

The Four Seasons

There are four seasons in a year. Spring is warm and flowers bloom. Summer is hot and we can swim. Autumn is cool and leaves fall from trees. Winter is cold and sometimes it snows. I like spring best because it is not too hot or cold. What is the weather like today? It is sunny and warm.

📚 生词提示：seasons 季节 | bloom 开花 | leaves 叶子 | fall 落下
