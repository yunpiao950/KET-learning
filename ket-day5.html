📖 Part 2: 语法知识点 (5 分钟)

形容词的位置与用法 (Adjectives)

用法结构例子
修饰名词形容词 + 名词a red apple, a big house
放在 be 动词后主语 + be + 形容词The sky is blue. / She is happy.
多个形容词大小 + 颜色 + 名词a big red apple
💡 小贴士：
  • 形容词没有复数形式
  • 形容词不放在名词后面
  • 询问颜色用 What color

🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My Clothes

Hello! I'm Sarah. Today I'm wearing a yellow T-shirt and a blue skirt. My shoes are white. They are new. My best friend Lily is wearing a red dress. She has a black hat too. It's very sunny today. I like summer because I can wear colorful clothes. My favorite color is pink, but I don't have a pink dress. My mother says she will buy me one for my birthday!

📚 生词提示：skirt 短裙 | sunny 晴朗的 | summer 夏天 | colorful 多彩的 | favorite 最喜欢的
