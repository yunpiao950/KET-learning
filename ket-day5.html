🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

At the Zoo

Today we go to the zoo. I see many animals! The elephant is big. It has a long nose. The monkey is funny. It eats bananas. The panda is cute. It is black and white. The lion is strong. The zebra has stripes. I love animals!

📚 生词提示：zoo 动物园 | funny 有趣的 | cute 可爱的
