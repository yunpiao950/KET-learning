🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My Town

I live in a small town. It is nice and quiet. My school is near my home. I walk to school every day. The library is next to the school. I read books there on Saturday. The hospital is on Main Street. My aunt works there. The bank is across from the post office. Mom goes to the bank on Friday. The restaurant is near the station. We eat there sometimes. The hotel is big. Many people stay there. I love my town!

📚 生词提示：quiet 安静的 | Main Street 大街 | across from 在...对面
