📖 Part 2: 语法知识点 (5 分钟)

可数名词与不可数名词 (Countable & Uncountable Nouns)

类型说明例子
可数名词可以用数字计数，有单复数an apple, two apples, a book, three books
不可数名词不能用数字直接计数，无复数water, milk, rice, bread, chicken
💡 小贴士：
  • 可数名词前可用 a/an/many
  • 不可数名词前可用 much/some/any
  • 询问可数名词用 How many，不可数名词用 How much

🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My Favorite Food

Hi! I'm Mike. I love food! For breakfast, I usually have bread and milk. Sometimes I eat eggs too. For lunch, I have rice and chicken at school. The chicken is very delicious! For dinner, my mother cooks noodles or fish. My favorite food is fish. I don't like vegetables very much, but my mother says they are good for me. I drink water every day. It's healthy!

📚 生词提示：usually 通常 | sometimes 有时 | delicious 美味的 | vegetables 蔬菜 | healthy 健康的

✍️ Part 4: 练习题 (10 分钟)

一、选择正确的词 (5 题)

1. I have ___ for breakfast. (A) a bread (B) bread (C) breads

2. How ___ water do you drink every day? (A) many (B) much (C) some

3. There are three ___ on the table. (A) apple (B) apples (C) an apple

4. She doesn't like ___ . (A) chicken (B) a chicken (C) chickens

5. How ___ students are in your class? (A) much (B) many (C) some

二、单项选择 (5 题)

6. I usually have breakfast ___ 7 o'clock. (A) in (B) on (C) at

7. We have lunch ___ school. (A) in (B) at (C) on

8. My mother cooks ___ dinner. (A) a (B) an (C) /

9. Fish ___ good for you. (A) is (B) are (C) be

10. I don't like vegetables, ___ my mother says they are healthy. (A) and (B) but (C) or

三、阅读理解 (5 题)

11. What does Mike have for breakfast? (A) Rice and chicken (B) Bread and milk (C) Noodles and fish
