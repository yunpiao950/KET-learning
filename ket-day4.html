📖 Part 2: 语法知识点 (5 分钟)

现在进行时 (Present Continuous)

类型结构例子
肯定句主语 + am/is/are + 动词-ingI am reading. / She is sleeping.
否定句主语 + am/is/are + not + 动词-ingI am not playing. / He isn't running.
疑问句Am/Is/Are + 主语 + 动词-ing？Are you listening? / Is she cooking?
💡 小贴士：
  • 现在进行时表示正在进行的动作
  • 常用时间状语：now, at the moment, look, listen
  • 动词-ing 形式：直接加 ing (play→playing)，去 e 加 ing (write→writing)，双写加 ing (run→running)

🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

At the Zoo

Today is Sunday. My family and I are at the zoo. Look! The elephants are eating bananas. They are very big. The monkeys are jumping in the trees. They are so funny! The pandas are sleeping. They are cute and black and white. The lions are walking. They are strong. The birds are singing in the trees. I am very happy today. I love animals!

📚 生词提示：zoo 动物园 | jumping 跳跃 | funny 有趣的 | cute 可爱的 | singing 唱歌
