🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

Look at Me

Look at my face. I have two big eyes. They are brown. I have a small nose. My mouth is not big. I have short hair. It is black. Look at my arms. They are long. I have two hands. Each hand has five fingers. Look at my legs. They are strong. I have two feet. I can run and jump. I can clap my hands. I can touch my head. This is me!

📚 生词提示：face 脸 | fingers 手指 | strong 强壮的 | touch 触摸
