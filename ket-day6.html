📖 Part 2: 语法知识点 (5 分钟)

时间的表达 (Telling Time)

表达结构例子
整点数字 + o'clock7 o'clock (7 点)
半点half past + 数字half past 7 (7 点半)
分钟分钟 + past/to + 小时10 past 7 (7:10) / 10 to 8 (7:50)
直接读小时 + 分钟7:30 (seven thirty)
💡 小贴士：
  • 询问时间用 What time is it?
  • 在几点用介词 at (at 7 o'clock)
  • 早上/下午/晚上用 in the morning/afternoon/evening

🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My Day

Hi! I'm David. I get up at 7 o'clock every morning. I have breakfast at 7:30. I go to school at 8 o'clock. I have lunch at 12 o'clock at school. School finishes at 4 o'clock in the afternoon. I do my homework at 5 o'clock. I have dinner at 6:30 in the evening. I go to bed at 9 o'clock. This is my day. It's busy but happy!

📚 生词提示：get up 起床 | finishes 结束 | homework 家庭作业 | busy 忙碌的
