🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My Body

Hello! Let me tell you about my body. I have one head and two eyes. I can see with my eyes. I have two ears and I can hear. I have one nose and one mouth. I have two arms and two hands. I can clap with my hands. I have two legs and two feet. I can run and jump. I have many teeth. I brush my teeth every day. My body is very useful. I take care of my body. Do you exercise every day? Exercise makes your body strong and healthy!

📚 生词提示：exercise 运动 | body 身体 | useful 有用的 | healthy 健康的
