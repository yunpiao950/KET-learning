🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My School

I am a student. I go to school every day from Monday to Friday. My school is big and beautiful. Our classroom is on the second floor. There are 30 students in our class. My teacher is very nice. She teaches us English and math. I have a desk and a chair. My desk is clean. I put my books and pens in my bag. I like my school. I study hard every day!

📚 生词提示：student 学生 | classroom 教室 | teacher 老师 | study 学习
