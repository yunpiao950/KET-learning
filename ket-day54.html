🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

Our New House

We have a new house. It is big. There are four rooms. The living room is big. We watch TV there. The kitchen is small. Mom cooks there. There are two bedrooms. One is for my parents. One is for me. My bedroom is blue. There is a bed and a table. The bathroom is near my room. There are two windows in the living room. The door is brown. I love our new house!

📚 生词提示：new 新的 | small 小的 | brown 棕色的
