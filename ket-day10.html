📖 Part 2: 语法知识点 (5 分钟)

可数/不可数名词

语法说明例子
可数/不可数名词an apple, some rice可数：an apple；不可数：some rice
💡 小贴士：
  • 可数：an apple；不可数：some rice

🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My Favorite Food

For breakfast, I have bread and milk. Sometimes I eat an egg too. For lunch, I eat rice and chicken. My mom makes delicious chicken! For dinner, we have fish and vegetables. I love fruits too. My favorite fruit is apples. An apple a day keeps the doctor away! Bananas and oranges are also yummy.

📚 生词提示：breakfast 早餐 | lunch 午餐 | dinner 晚餐 | delicious 美味的
