📖 Part 2: 语法知识点 (5 分钟)

询问与回答职业 (Asking about Jobs)

句型例子
询问职业What do you do? / What's your job? / What does he/she do?
回答职业I'm a teacher. / He's a doctor. / She's a nurse.
工作地点Where do you work? / I work in a school/hospital.
工作内容What do you do at work? / I teach/help people.
💡 小贴士：
  • 职业前用不定冠词 a/an (a teacher, an artist)
  • 第三人称单数用 does 提问：What does he do?
  • 某些职业有性别区分：policeman/policewoman, actor/actress

🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My Family's Jobs

Hello! I'm Amy. Let me tell you about my family's jobs. My father is a doctor. He works in a big hospital. He helps sick people every day. My mother is a teacher. She teaches English at a primary school. She loves her students. My uncle is a policeman. He works hard to keep our city safe. My aunt is a nurse. She works in the same hospital as my father. I want to be a singer when I grow up because I love singing. What do you want to be in the future?

📚 生词提示：sick 生病的 | primary school 小学 | safe 安全的 | grow up 长大 | future 未来

✍️ Part 4: 练习题 (10 分钟)

一、选择正确的冠词 (5 题)

1. She is ___ teacher. (A) a (B) an (C) the

2. He wants to be ___ artist. (A) a (B) an (C) the

3. My father is ___ doctor. (A) a (B) an (C) /

4. She works as ___ nurse. (A) a (B) an (C) the

5. I want to be ___ singer. (A) a (B) an (C) the

二、单项选择 (5 题)

6. What ___ your father do? (A) do (B) does (C) is

7. Where ___ you work? (A) do (B) does (C) are

8. A doctor works in a ___ . (A) school (B) hospital (C) farm

9. A teacher ___ students. (A) teach (B) teaches (C) teaching

10. A policeman catches ___ . (A) doctors (B) thieves (C) teachers

三、阅读理解 (5 题)

11. What is Amy's father's job? (A) Teacher (B) Doctor (C) Policeman
