📖 Part 2: 语法知识点 (5 分钟)

穿着表达

语法说明例子
wear/put onwear 穿着状态；put on 穿上动作I wear a coat. Put on your shoes.
💡 小贴士：
  • wear 表示穿着的状态；put on 表示穿上的动作

🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

Getting Dressed

Today is Monday. I get up at seven o'clock. I put on my clothes. I wear a blue T-shirt and black trousers. My sister wears a red dress and white shoes. It is cold today. Mom says, "Put on your jacket!" I put on my blue jacket. My brother wears a green shirt and brown trousers. He puts on his coat and hat. We wear socks and shoes. Then we have breakfast and go to school. We look nice in our clothes!

📚 生词提示：o'clock 点钟 | nice 好看的
