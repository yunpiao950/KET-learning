📖 Part 2: 语法知识点 (5 分钟)

like/love/enjoy + 动词-ing

动词用法例子
like喜欢 (程度一般)I like playing football.
love喜爱 (程度强烈)I love reading books.
enjoy享受 (很喜爱)She enjoys dancing.
hate讨厌He hates getting up early.
💡 小贴士：
  • 这些动词后面都接动词-ing 形式
  • 询问爱好用 What's your hobby? 或 What do you like doing?
  • play + 球类运动 (play football, play basketball)

🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My Hobbies

Hello! I'm Emma. I have many hobbies. I like playing basketball with my friends after school. It's very exciting! I also enjoy swimming. I go to the swimming pool every Saturday. My best friend Lily loves dancing. She takes dancing lessons twice a week. My brother Tom likes football. He plays football every Sunday. In the evening, I enjoy reading books before bed. Reading is relaxing. What's your hobby?

📚 生词提示：exciting 令人兴奋的 | pool 游泳池 | lessons 课程 | twice 两次 | relaxing 放松的

✍️ Part 4: 练习题 (10 分钟)

一、用动词的适当形式填空 (5 题)

1. I like ___ (play) basketball.

2. She enjoys ___ (read) books.

3. They love ___ (swim) in summer.

4. He hates ___ (get) up early.

5. We enjoy ___ (dance) at the party.

二、单项选择 (5 题)

6. I like playing ___ . (A) the football (B) football (C) a football

7. She is good ___ basketball. (A) at (B) in (C) on

8. ___ is your hobby? (A) What (B) How (C) Where

9. He plays football ___ Sunday. (A) in (B) at (C) on

10. Swimming is good ___ . (A) exercise (B) exercising (C) to exercise

三、阅读理解 (5 题)

11. What does Emma like playing? (A) Football (B) Basketball (C) Tennis

12. When does Emma go swimming? (A) Every Sunday (B) Every Saturday (C) Every Friday

13. What is Lily's hobby? (A) Swimming (B) Reading (C) Dancing

14. How often does Lily take dancing lessons? (A) Once a week (B) Twice a week (C) Three times a week

15. What does Emma do before bed? (A) Watch TV (B) Read books (C) Listen to music
