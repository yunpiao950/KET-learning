📖 Part 2: 语法知识点 (5 分钟)

冠词 (Articles): a/an/the

类型用法例子
a用于辅音音素开头的单词前a book, a pen, a desk
an用于元音音素开头的单词前an apple, an eraser, an hour
the特指某个人或物The teacher is in the classroom.
💡 小贴士：
  • a/an 用于泛指，the 用于特指
  • 第一次提到用 a/an，再次提到用 the
  • 独一无二的东西前用 the (the sun, the moon)

🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My School Day

Hello! My name is Lucy. I am 10 years old. I go to Green Primary School. My classroom is on the second floor. It is big and clean. There are 25 students in my class. My teacher's name is Miss Wang. She teaches us English. I have a desk and a chair. My desk is blue. I have many books and pencils. I like my school very much!

📚 生词提示：primary 小学的 | clean 干净的 | teaches 教 (teach 的第三人称单数)

✍️ Part 4: 练习题 (10 分钟)

一、选择正确的冠词 (a/an/the) (5 题)

1. I have ___ apple in my bag. (A) a (B) an (C) the

2. ___ sun is very hot today. (A) A (B) An (C) The

3. She is ___ teacher. (A) a (B) an (C) the

4. Can you pass me ___ pen on the desk? (A) a (B) an (C) the

5. He wants to buy ___ umbrella. (A) a (B) an (C) the

二、单项选择 (5 题)

6. There ___ 25 students in my class. (A) is (B) are (C) am

7. My classroom ___ on the second floor. (A) is (B) are (C) be

8. She ___ English very well. (A) teach (B) teaches (C) teaching

9. I go to school ___ day. (A) every (B) all (C) each

10. This is ___ book. (A) I (B) my (C) me

三、阅读理解 (5 题)

11. How old is Lucy? (A) 9 (B) 10 (C) 11

12. What is the name of Lucy's school? (A) Red Primary School (B) Green Primary School (C) Blue Primary School

13. Where is the classroom? (A) First floor (B) Second floor (C) Third floor

14. How many students are in the class? (A) 20 (B) 25 (C) 30

15. What color is Lucy's desk? (A) Red (B) Blue (C) Green
