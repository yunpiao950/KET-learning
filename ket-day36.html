🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

On the Farm

My uncle has a farm. There are many animals on the farm. The cows are big. They give us milk. The sheep are white. They have wool. The horses are strong. They run fast. The pigs are fat. They sleep a lot. The chickens are small. They lay eggs. I also have a pet dog. His name is Spot. He is black and white. He can run and jump. He is my good friend. I love the farm!

📚 生词提示：uncle 叔叔 | pigs 猪 | wool 羊毛 | lay eggs 下蛋
