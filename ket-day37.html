语法说明例子

💡 小贴士：
  • There is + 单数；There are + 复数

🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

In the Park

There is a beautiful park near my home. There are many trees in the park. The trees are tall and green. There is a lake in the middle. The water is clean. There are fish in the lake. There is a small mountain behind the lake. There are flowers on the mountain. They are red, yellow and blue. There is a forest near the park. Many birds live there. The sky is blue. The sun is shining. I love nature. I go to the park every weekend!

📚 生词提示：beautiful 美丽的 | middle 中间 | shining 照耀
