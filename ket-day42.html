🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My Computer

I have a computer at home. It is on my desk. I use it every day. I use it for homework. I also use it for games. My computer has a big screen. The keyboard is black. I have a tablet too. I use my tablet to read books. My dad uses his phone for work. He sends emails every day. My mom uses her phone to take photos. We all use technology. The internet is very useful. We can learn many things online. Technology makes life easier!

📚 生词提示：desk 书桌 | useful 有用的 | online 在线
