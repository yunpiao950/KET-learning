语法说明例子

  • 第三人称单数动词加 s: He gets up early.

🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My Daily Routine

I get up at seven o'clock every morning. I wash my face and brush my teeth. Then I have breakfast with my family. I go to school at eight. I have four lessons in the morning. I have lunch at school at twelve. In the afternoon, I have two lessons. I go home at four. I do my homework first. Then I play with my dog. We have dinner at six. I watch TV for half an hour. I go to bed at nine o'clock. This is my daily routine!

📚 生词提示：wash 洗 | brush 刷 | lessons 课程
