🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

Staying Healthy

Health is very important. To stay healthy, we need to do many things. First, eat healthy food. Eat more fruit and vegetables. Drink water and milk. Don't eat too much chocolate or candy. Second, do exercise every day. Run, swim, or play ball games. Exercise makes us strong. Third, sleep well. Go to bed early and get up early. Children need ten hours of sleep. Fourth, wash hands often. It keeps germs away. When you are sick, see a doctor. Take medicine and have a rest. Don't go to school. Stay at home and get better soon!

📚 生词提示：candy 糖果 | germs 细菌 | sick 生病的
