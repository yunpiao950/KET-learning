📖 Part 2: 语法知识点 (5 分钟)

家庭成员表达

语法说明例子
have/has + 家庭成员I have a brother, She has two sisters表达有某个家庭成员
💡 小贴士：
  • live with 和...住在一起；visit 拜访；tell stories 讲故事

🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My Family

I have a big family. There are six people in my family. My father is a teacher. He is tall and kind. My mother is a doctor. She is beautiful. I have one brother. He is older than me. I have one sister. She is younger than me. My grandpa lives with us. He tells us stories. My grandma lives nearby. She visits us on Sundays. I love my family!

📚 生词提示：people 人 | teacher 老师 | doctor 医生 | older 更大的 | younger 更小的 | nearby 附近
