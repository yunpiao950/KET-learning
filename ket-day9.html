📖 Part 2: 语法知识点 (5 分钟)

天气表达法 (Talking about Weather)

句型例子
询问天气What's the weather like? / How's the weather?
回答天气It's sunny/hot/cold. / It's a sunny day.
询问温度What's the temperature?
回答温度It's 20 degrees.
💡 小贴士：
  • 天气用 it 作主语：It's raining. / It's sunny.
  • 季节前用介词 in：in spring, in summer
  • 天气形容词：sun→sunny, cloud→cloudy, rain→rainy, wind→windy, snow→snowy

🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

The Four Seasons

There are four seasons in a year. Spring is from March to May. It's warm and sometimes rainy. Flowers bloom in spring. Summer is from June to August. It's hot and sunny. I like swimming in summer. Autumn is from September to November. It's cool and windy. The leaves turn yellow and red. Winter is from December to February. It's cold and sometimes snowy. I can make a snowman in winter. My favorite season is spring because it's not too hot or too cold.

📚 生词提示：bloom 开花 | leaves 树叶 (leaf 的复数) | turn 变得 | snowman 雪人 | favorite 最喜欢的
