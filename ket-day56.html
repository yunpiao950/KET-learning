🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

Sports Day

Today is our school sports day. It is sunny. We are on the playground. I run in the race. I run fast. My friend Tom jumps high. He wins first place. My sister plays tennis. She wins her game. My brother plays football. His team wins. I swim in the pool. I come second. We ride bikes after the races. We are tired but happy. Sports day is fun. I love sports!

📚 生词提示：race 比赛 | playground 操场 | first place 第一名 | second 第二名
