🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My Family

There are five people in my family. My grandpa is seventy years old. My grandma is sixty-eight. My dad is forty. My mom is thirty-eight. I am ten years old. We live in a house. It has three bedrooms. One is for my grandparents. One is for my parents. One is for me. We have two bathrooms. Our house has four windows in the front. There are six rooms in total. I love my family!

📚 生词提示：total 总共 | windows 窗户 | bedrooms 卧室
