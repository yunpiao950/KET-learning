🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My Colorful Room

My room is very colorful. The walls are white. The floor is brown. My bed has a blue blanket. My desk is green. I have a red chair. My curtains are yellow. They are bright. My toy box is purple. My rug is pink and soft. I have a black lamp. It gives warm light. My room is orange too. One wall is orange. I love my colorful room!

📚 生词提示：colorful 多彩的 | walls 墙壁 | curtains 窗帘 | rug 地毯 | lamp 台灯
