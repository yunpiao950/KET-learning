🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My Town

I live in a small town. There are many places here. I go to school every day. My school is near my home. On weekends, I go to the park. I play football there. My mom goes to the shop. She buys food. My dad goes to work. He works in an office. On Sundays, we go to the restaurant. We eat together. Sometimes we go to the cinema. We watch movies. I love my town!

📚 生词提示：town 城镇 | near 附近 | weekends 周末 | office 办公室 | together 一起
