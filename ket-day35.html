📖 Part 2: 语法知识点 (5 分钟)

like + doing

语法说明例子
like + doingI like swimming. She likes reading.表达喜欢做某事
💡 小贴士：
  • like/love/enjoy + doing; 第三人称单数 likes

🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

Our Hobbies

We all have different hobbies. I like collecting stamps. I have stamps from many countries. My sister likes taking photos. She has a camera. My brother likes playing football. He plays every day. My mom likes reading books. She reads in the evening. My dad likes listening to music. He plays the guitar. We all have fun with our hobbies. Hobbies make us happy!

📚 生词提示：different 不同的 | camera 相机 | guitar 吉他
