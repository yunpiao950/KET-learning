🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

How I Go to School

I go to school from Monday to Friday. I usually go to school by bus. The bus stop is near my home. The bus ride takes twenty minutes. Sometimes my dad drives me to school in his car. It is faster than the bus. On sunny days, I ride my bike to school. It is good exercise. My friend Tom lives far from school. He comes by train. His sister goes to work by underground. In the holiday, we go to visit my grandma. We go by plane. It takes two hours. I like traveling by plane. It is fast and fun!

📚 生词提示：usually 通常 | exercise 锻炼 | traveling 旅行
