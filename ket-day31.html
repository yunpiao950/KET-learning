🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My Weekend

I love the weekend! On Saturday morning, I get up late. I have breakfast at nine. Then I do my homework. In the afternoon, I go to the park with my friends. We play football there. In the evening, we go to the cinema. We watch a funny movie. On Sunday, I visit my grandma with my parents. We have lunch together. Grandma makes delicious food. In the afternoon, I read books at home. I relax and rest. Sunday evening, I get ready for school. I love my weekend!

📚 生词提示：late 晚 | movie 电影 | ready 准备好的
