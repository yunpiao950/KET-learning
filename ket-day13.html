🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

House

Welcome to my house! Our house is new and clean. There are four rooms in my house. My bedroom is small but cozy. There is a bed, a table and a chair in my room. The kitchen is big. My mom cooks there every day. The bathroom is very clean. We have a beautiful garden too. There are many flowers in the garden. I love my home!
