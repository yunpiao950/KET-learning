🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

The Weather Today

Today is Tuesday. The weather is very changeable. In the morning, it is sunny and warm. I go to school with my sister. At noon, it becomes cloudy. The sky is gray. In the afternoon, it starts to rain. It is rainy and windy. I put on my coat. After school, the rain stops. It is cool and cloudy. My brother says, "Tomorrow will be sunny!" I hope so. I like sunny days. We can play outside. But rainy days are good too. I can read books at home!

📚 生词提示：changeable 多变的 | gray 灰色的 | hope 希望
