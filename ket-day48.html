🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

Four Seasons

I love all four seasons. In spring, it is warm and sunny. Flowers bloom. In summer, it is hot. Sometimes it is rainy. I go swimming. In autumn, it is cool and windy. Leaves turn yellow and red. In winter, it is cold. Sometimes it is snowy. I wear a coat and hat. My favorite season is spring. The weather is nice. I can play outside. Every season is special!

📚 生词提示：seasons 季节 | bloom 开花 | leaves 叶子 (leaf 的复数)
