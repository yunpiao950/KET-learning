🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My Town

I live in a small town. There are many places here. I go to school every day. It is near my home. On weekends, I play in the park with my friends. There is a big library. I read books there on Saturday. My mom goes to the shop for food. My dad goes to the bank on Friday. When we are sick, we go to the hospital. The doctor is kind. Sometimes we go to the cinema. We watch movies there. On Sunday, we eat at a restaurant. I love my town!

📚 生词提示：town 小镇 | near 靠近 | kind 和蔼的
