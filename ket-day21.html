🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

The Weather Today

Today is Monday. The weather is sunny and warm. The sky is blue. There are no clouds. It is a nice day. Yesterday was rainy and cold. I stayed at home all day. Tomorrow will be cloudy. Maybe it will rain. In summer, it is usually hot and sunny. In winter, it is cold and sometimes snowy. Spring is warm and beautiful. Autumn is cool and windy. I like sunny days best. On sunny days, I can play outside with my friends. We go to the park and play games!

📚 生词提示：clouds 云 | usually 通常 | outside 外面
