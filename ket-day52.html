🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My Big Family

I have a big family. My father is a teacher. He works at a school. My mother is a doctor. She works at a hospital. I have one brother and one sister. My brother is older. My sister is younger. My grandpa lives with us. He tells me stories. My grandma lives nearby. She visits us on Sundays. I have two uncles and three aunts. I have four cousins. We play together. I love my big family!

📚 生词提示：older 年长的 | younger 年幼的 | nearby 附近 | visits 拜访
