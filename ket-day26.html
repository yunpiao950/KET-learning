🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

Healthy Eating

Food is important for our health. For breakfast, I have bread and milk. Sometimes I eat an egg. For lunch, I have rice and meat with vegetables. My mom makes delicious soup too. I drink water and juice. I don't drink cola. It is not healthy. For dinner, we have noodles or salad. I like fruit very much. Apples, bananas and oranges are my favorites. I don't like chocolate very much. It is too sweet. Eating healthy food makes me strong. I eat three meals every day!

📚 生词提示：healthy 健康的 | cola 可乐 | strong 强壮的
