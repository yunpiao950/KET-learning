🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

My Pets

I have two pets. One is a dog and the other is a cat. My dog's name is Max. He is brown and very friendly. He likes to run in the park. My cat's name is Mimi. She is white and black. She likes to sleep on the sofa. I feed them every day. Max eats dog food and Mimi eats fish. I love my pets very much. They are my best friends!

📚 生词提示：friendly 友好的 | feed 喂养 | best friends 最好的朋友
