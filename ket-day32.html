🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

Going Shopping

Today is Sunday. Mom and I go shopping. We go to the supermarket. It is big and clean. We need to buy food. We buy some bread, milk and eggs. Mom buys vegetables and fruit. I want to buy a toy. The toy car is nice but expensive. It is fifty yuan. The ball is cheap. It is ten yuan. I buy the ball. Mom pays by card. We put things in the bag. Then we go home. We have a happy shopping day!

📚 生词提示：supermarket 超市 | clean 干净的 | toy 玩具
