🎧 Part 3: 阅读理解 (5 分钟)

Places

There are many interesting places in my city. There is a big park near my home. I play there on weekends. The zoo is far but fun. We can see many animals there. The library is quiet. I read books there. The cinema is exciting. I watch movies there. The restaurant has delicious food. The hospital helps sick people. I love my city!
